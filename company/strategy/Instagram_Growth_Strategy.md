## PART 10: Common Mistakes to Avoid

| Mistake | Why It's Bad | Fix |
|---------|---|---|
| Posting too much (10+ posts/day) | Followers get annoyed, algorithm throttles | Stick to 4-5 posts/week |
| No captions (just images) | Algorithm prioritizes engagement; no engagement = low reach | Write 3-5 sentence captions always |
| Hashtags in first comment | Hidden from algorithm | Put hashtags directly in caption |
| No call-to-action | People see content but don't know what to do | Always ask: "DM me", "save this", "tag a friend" |
| Inconsistent posting | Algorithm likes consistency; drops in frequency = drops in reach | Batch-create content, post on schedule |
| Ignoring comments | People unfollow when not acknowledged | Respond to all comments within 4 hours |
| Begging for follows | Looks desperate, algorithm penalizes | Focus on value; follows come naturally |
| Same caption every time | Boring, no personality | Vary tone, tell stories, ask questions |
| Posting at random times | Miss peak engagement windows | Post Tue-Sat, 11 AM or 3 PM |
