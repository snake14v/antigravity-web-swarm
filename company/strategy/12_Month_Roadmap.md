## Risks + Contingencies

### Risk 1: Market Doesn't Adopt (Probability: 20%)
- **Mitigation:** Start with small pilots, gather feedback, pivot early
- **Contingency:** Shift to OLOG Platform or Web Design revenue

### Risk 2: Hardware Supply Chain Issues
- **Mitigation:** Pre-order components, build stock buffer
- **Contingency:** Use local suppliers, hand-assembly if needed

### Risk 3: Competition Emerges
- **Mitigation:** Build moat (GitHub community, brand, data)
- **Contingency:** Focus on service + support differentiation

### Risk 4: Fundraising Falls Through
- **Mitigation:** Bootstrap aggressively, focus on profitability
- **Contingency:** Slow growth to ₹500K MRR by Dec, plan Series A in 2027

### Risk 5: Founder Burnout
- **Mitigation:** Hire early, take regular breaks, set boundaries
- **Contingency:** Part-time CEO role, hire operations co-founder
