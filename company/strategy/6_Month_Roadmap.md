## MONTHS 4-6: Q3 (July—September)
## The Scaling Quarter — 3x Revenue, Build Small Team

### Month 4: July (Scale Sales to 6 Customers)

#### Week 1-2: Demand Capture
- **Goal:** Launch aggressive sales campaign
- **Method:** Referral program + cold outreach + LinkedIn ads (if budget allows)
- **Target:** 15 qualified leads by July 15

**Referral Program:**
- Offer: ₹2,500 per referred customer who pays (customer #1-3 are eligible)
- Target: 5 referrals (₹12,500 spend, but get ₹125K revenue)
- WhatsApp template: "Know another shop owner who'd want ShopSense? Refer them, get ₹2,500."

**Cold Outreach:**
- Identify 50 chai shops in Bangalore (via Google Maps, personal research)
- 3-step WhatsApp sequence:
  - Day 1: "Hi [Name], saw your shop. We built AI for shops like yours. Would love to show you → [link]"
  - Day 5: "Checking in — any questions about ShopSense?"
  - Day 10: "Last message — free demo this week?"
- Expected conversion: 10% (5 conversations → 2 customers)

**LinkedIn Ads (Optional):**
- Budget: ₹10,000 for July (test)
- Target: Bangalore business owners age 30-50
- CTA: "ShopSense demo — free this week"
- Expected: 20-30 clicks, 2-4 conversions

- **Install:** 3 new ShopSense units (customers #4, #5, #6)
- **Per-install:** 4 hours on-site, 1 week post-install support
- **Outcomes:**
  - 6 total deployments (3 from Q2 + 3 new)
