## Failure Modes + Mitigation

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|-----------|
| Can't find chai shop owners | No pilots | Start with friends' shops, ask for referrals |
| Hardware doesn't work in field | Pilot fails | Test in 5+ different shops before official deploy |
| LinkedIn posts get no engagement | No awareness | Follow 10+ people daily, comment on posts, use trending audio |
| GitHub stars stay at 5 | No credibility | Post to HN, Reddit, tag YOLOv8 community |
| Burnout (solo founder) | Quit | Block Sundays for rest, aim for 5-6 hour days max |

## Notes for Self (Vaishak)

- **Be ruthless about time:** Each day has a priority. If a task isn't in the roadmap, it's a distraction.
- **Ship weekly:** Every Friday, push something to GitHub or post something public.
- **Follow up obsessively:** Of your 3 chai shop contacts, follow up 3x each. Someone will say yes.
- **Document everything:** Every deploy, every conversation, every failure. This is your case study.
- **Don't over-engineer:** The ₹25K hardware should just work. No fancy UI. No 10 features. Do 3 things really well.
- **Revenue is the signal:** Pilots are validations. A paid customer is proof. Go after paid.
