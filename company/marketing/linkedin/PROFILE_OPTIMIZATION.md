## 6. ENGAGEMENT STRATEGY

To boost LinkedIn algorithm visibility and build authentic network effects:

### Type 1: Thoughtful Comments (2-3x per week)
- **Where:** Reply to posts from VCs, founders, tech leaders in your space (edge AI, startups, Bangalore tech)
- **What:** Add 2-3 sentence comments that show you've actually read the post and can add specificity
- **Example format:** "This is why we made [decision] on ShopSense — [specific reasoning]. Curious if you've seen [related trend] in your experience?"
- **Why:** Comments get ~3x more engagement than likes. You'll get profile visits from engaged audiences.

### Type 2: Shipping Updates (1-2x per week)
- **What:** Status updates on your actual work. Keep them short, honest, specific.
- **Examples:**
  - "Just shipped OLOG's logistics dashboard. Now live with 3 customers. 42 bugs to go 😅"
  - "ShopSense now runs YOLOv8 nano on RPi in <200ms. Edge AI really works."
  - "Hiring our first customer for AurumGuard. DMs open."
- **Why:** Founders follow founders. These posts get views from investors, customers, potential cofounders.

### Type 3: Technical Learning Posts (1x per week)
- **What:** Share something you learned while building. Keep it specific, not preachy.
- **Examples:**
  - "Why we ditched cloud logging for local-first architectures in AurumGuard"
  - "How we reduced YOLOv8 inference from 400ms to 180ms on Raspberry Pi"
  - "Firebase real-time subscriptions at 10K concurrent users: what breaks first"
- **Why:** Position you as someone who ships AND knows deeply. Engineers and founders engage on technical authenticity.

### Engagement Tactics:
- **Respond to every comment** on your posts within 24 hours. Signals active founder.
- **Follow 20-30 relevant people weekly** (Bangalore founders, YOLOv8/edge AI practitioners, logistics founders, VC associates interested in hardware).
- **Repost 1-2 good external posts weekly** with your take. This amplifies your network and signals taste.
