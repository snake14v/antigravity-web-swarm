## PROPOSED SOLUTION: ShopSense

### What is ShopSense?
An AI-powered edge camera system that sits above your billing counter and:
1. **Watches every transaction in real-time**
2. **Catches billing errors** (wrong amount, missing item, duplicate charge) before the customer pays
3. **Alerts staff immediately** with a small screen/audio alert
4. **Stores data locally** (works without internet)
5. **Provides daily reports** showing accuracy, speed, and error patterns

### Hardware You'll Get
**Starter Kit (₹25K) - Recommended for single-counter shops:**
- 2x AI camera units (720p, edge-processor, WiFi + offline capable)
- 2x power adapters & cable harnesses
- 1x counter mount stand (adjustable angle)
- 1x backup WiFi router (ensures reliability)
- Quick-start guide + training (staff gets 20-min walkthrough)

**Pro Kit (₹35K) - For multi-counter or high-volume shops:**
- 4x AI camera units (360° coverage)
- Same accessories as Starter
- Advanced analytics dashboard
- Priority support

- **Result:** System is live and tracking by noon
