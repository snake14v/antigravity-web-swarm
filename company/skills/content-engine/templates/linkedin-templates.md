## Template 4: Edge AI Explainer

### Hook
"Edge AI isn't new. We just built it for chai shops instead of data centers."

### Body
Here's what edge AI actually means (without the jargon):

Your system learns and decides on the device itself. No data flying to the cloud. No lag. No need to be online.

For retail, this changes everything:
- Inventory gets smarter as you use it (learns your patterns)
- Billing works offline, full stop
- Your data stays yours
- Speed: instant, no waiting for a server 3,000 km away

Most AI is built for scale (Facebook's billions of users). Edge AI is built for constraint (Hari's chai shop in Rajajinagar with 20 mbps broadband, interrupted).

When you design for constraint, you design for resilience. That's the insight.

## Template 9: Thought Leadership on AI in Retail

### Hook
"AI in retail doesn't need to be smart. It needs to be reliable."

### Body
There's a trap in building AI for retail:

You see the fancy use cases: predictive demand forecasting, customer lifetime value models, dynamic pricing. So you build for those.

But then you ship to a 2,000 sq ft shop owner with 45 SKUs and no data scientist.

What they actually need:
- "Tell me what's about to run out"
- "Show me what sold yesterday vs today"
- "Flag items that don't move"

These aren't sexy problems. But they're 80% of the value.

We spent months building predictive demand forecasting. One shop owner asked: "Can you just tell me when I'm about to run out so I don't disappoint customers?"

Boom. That feature gets used 10x more than our fancy model.

The lesson: AI for SMBs is about reliability + simplicity, not cutting-edge models. Design for the owner who works 12 hours, not the data science PhD.

## Usage Tips

- **Timing**: Post Tue 10 AM or Thu 2 PM IST (test and refine for your audience)
- **Personalization**: Replace shop names/owner names with real ones you've spoken to (always ask permission)
- **Length**: Aim for 150-250 words (LinkedIn favors complete reads)
- **Emoji sparingly**: 1 emoji max, and only if it feels natural
- **Test variants**: Try same template with different hooks; track engagement
- **Follow-up**: Reply to every comment within 4 hours (builds community, signals engagement)
