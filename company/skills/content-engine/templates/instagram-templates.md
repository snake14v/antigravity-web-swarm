## General Instagram Strategy

### Posting Schedule (Weekly)
- **Mon**: Reel OR Behind-the-scenes
- **Wed**: Carousel (feature/data/guide) OR Testimonial
- **Fri**: Meme OR Bangalore shop story

### Engagement Best Practices
- Reply to ALL comments within 1 hour
- Use 5-7 words max per reply (keep it snappy)
- Always ask a follow-up question in replies
- Save/share rate = more important than likes
- Stories (3x/week): quick polls, shop updates, product sneak peeks

### Content Mix by Week
- Week 1: 1 reel, 1 carousel, 1 story series
- Week 2: 1 behind-the-scenes, 1 testimonial, 1 story series
- Week 3: 1 meme, 1 Bangalore shop, 1 story series
- Week 4: 1 reel, 1 feature carousel, 1 story series

### Link Strategy
- Use Linktree or single "link in bio" (track clicks with UTM)
- Rotate: demo signup → blog post → product page → case study
- Track which posts drive most traffic via UTM codes
- A/B test: "Link in bio" vs specific CTAs

### Visual Consistency
- Colors: Navy/chai brown/green (consistent across posts)
- Fonts: Inter/Lato for headers, readable on mobile
- Aspect ratios: Always vertical (1080x1350px) or Stories (1080x1920px)
- Filters: Warm, slightly elevated contrast (no Instagram filters needed)
