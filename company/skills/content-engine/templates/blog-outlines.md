## SEO & Promotion Strategy

### Per-Post Effort
1. **Keyword research** (2 hours): Confirm primary, secondary, long-tail keywords
2. **Content draft** (6-8 hours): Write, link internally, optimize
3. **Brand review** (1-2 hours): Check voice, accuracy, CTA
4. **SEO optimization** (1.5 hours): Check checklist, optimize meta, images
5. **Promotion design** (3-4 hours): Create 3 LinkedIn posts, 5 Instagram assets, 2 WhatsApp messages
6. **Publishing** (1 hour): Schedule across channels
7. **First week engagement** (2-3 hours): Monitor comments, respond, promote organically

### Promotion Timeline (Per Post)
- **Monday-Tuesday**: Publish blog post, begin organic social promotion
- **Tuesday**: 2 LinkedIn teasers + Instagram carousel launch
- **Wednesday**: Behind-the-scenes content, WhatsApp message
- **Thursday**: LinkedIn stat/quote post + Instagram reel or story
- **Friday**: Final social push, email (if newsletter exists)

### SEO Success Metrics (Per Post)
- Week 1: 100-200 organic views
- Week 4: 300-500 organic views (cumulative)
- Week 12: 600-1000 organic views (steady-state)
- Goal: Each post contributes 5-10 qualified leads per month by month 6
