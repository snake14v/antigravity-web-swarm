## TEMPLATE: NEW VENDOR ONBOARDING CHECKLIST

- [ ] Verify business credentials (GST certificate, bank details, address)

- [ ] Negotiate payment terms (Net 30 preferred)
- [ ] Confirm MOQ, lead time, delivery cost
- [ ] Request sample (if applicable); inspect quality
- [ ] Establish backup supplier (for critical components)
- [ ] Add to procurement system with reorder thresholds
- [ ] Document contact person + escalation path
- [ ] Schedule quarterly scorecard reviews
- [ ] Create vendor agreement (for high-value suppliers >₹50K/year)
