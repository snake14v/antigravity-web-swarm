## BRAND VOICE (DO'S & DON'Ts)

### DO
✅ Use specific numbers (₹25K, 40% reduction, ₹2K/month savings)
✅ Lead with outcome ("save ₹2K/month") not feature ("real-time tracking")
✅ Respect the hustle (acknowledge 12-hour days, tight margins)
✅ Use first names, be warm and direct
✅ Admit limitations ("doesn't handle cold coffee")
✅ Use local cultural references (Bangalore chai culture, neighborhoods)

### DON'T
❌ Use corporate jargon (synergies, paradigms, optimize)
❌ Say "world-class" or "best-in-class" without specifics
❌ Oversell ("we're not replacing Khatabook tomorrow")
❌ Be formal (Mr./Mrs., corporate greetings)
❌ Use tech buzzwords without explaining (edge computing = runs offline)
❌ Make promises we can't keep ("100% accuracy")

**Tone:** Direct, practical, warm. All three always.

## COMMON Q&A (QUICK ANSWERS)

**"Why ₹25K for hardware?"**
- Investment-level pricing (not an expense)
- Saves ₹2K/month in waste → breaks even in 12 months
- 20x cheaper than RetailNext alternatives

**"How is ShopSense different from Khatabook?"**
- Khatabook = billing + accounting (requires manual inventory)
- ShopSense = operations layer (automatic inventory, waste detection, staff insights)
- They complement each other

**"Does it work without internet?"**
- Yes, 100% offline. Syncs to cloud when WiFi available.
- No cloud dependency = faster, cheaper, more reliable

**"How long to deploy?"**
- 2–3 hours total (unbox, mount cameras, train staff)
- Ready to use same day

**"What about accuracy?"**
- YOLOv8n model: 95%+ accuracy (trained on 500+ chai shop images)
- More accurate than manual counting (which is error-prone)
- We validate & adjust in Week 1

**"I'm not in Bangalore"**
- "We're launching in Bangalore first. Once we have 20 customers here, we'll expand to other cities."
