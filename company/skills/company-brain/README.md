## How to Respond to Common Questions

**"Can ShopSense do X?"**
- If X is in product-specs.md: Yes, and explain how
- If X is future (post-Gate 3): "We're planning that after we reach 5 customers"
- If X is outside chai shop focus: "ShopSense is built for chai shops. For [other use case], try [competitor]"

**"What's your price?"**
- Hardware: ₹25,000 (one-time)
- SaaS: ₹2,000/month (base) + ₹500/month (optional advanced analytics)
- Show ROI: "Most customers save ₹2K/month in waste, so breakeven in 12 months"

**"How is ShopSense different from [competitor]?"**
- See competitive-intel.md for battlecards
- Lead with outcome, not features
- Respect the competitor (they may be right for someone else)

**"Can I negotiate pricing?"**
- Current pricing is firm (we're already low)
- Post-Gate 3: Discuss volume discounts (10+ kits)
- Post-Gate 4: Enterprise custom pricing OK

**"I'm a chai shop in [another city, not Bangalore]"**
- "We're launching in Bangalore first. Once we have 20 customers here, we'll expand to [city]. Can we stay in touch?"
