# 🎨 Ooru Logix — Brand Guidelines

## Brand Identity

### Name
- **Full:** Ooru Logix
- **Meaning:** "Ooru" (ಊರು) = city/village/place in Kannada | "Logix" = logic/intelligence/systems
- **Tagline:** `Intelligence that understands your neighbourhood.`
- **Secondary:** `AI + Hardware for Indian Retail`

### Logo
- Primary: Black monochrome (circuit board + city skyline + growth arrow)
- Usage: Dark backgrounds with neon glow, light backgrounds with solid black
- Clear space: Minimum 20% of logo height on all sides
- Minimum size: 32px height (digital), 10mm (print)

## Voice & Tone

### Core Principles
1. **Engineering-authentic** — We speak like builders, not marketers
2. **No buzzwords** — Say what we mean. "Camera that detects cigarette brands" not "AI-powered smart retail intelligence solution"
3. **Local context** — Reference Bangalore, real shops, real problems
4. **Technically precise** — Include specs, measurements, ms timings
5. **Humble confidence** — We know what we built is good, but we're not arrogant

### Do Say
- "We built this"
- "It runs on a $35 computer"
- "No cloud, no monthly fee"
- "~380ms inference on a Pi"
- "Open source, MIT licensed"
- "Made for a shop that serves 200+ customers a day"

### Don't Say
- "Revolutionary AI platform"
- "Disrupting the retail industry"
- "State-of-the-art solution"
- "Leveraging cutting-edge technology"
- "Synergistic approach"
- "Our proprietary algorithm"

## Visual Style

### Photography
- Raw, unfiltered hardware shots
- Real chai shop environments (not staged)
- Close-ups of components, wiring, PCBs
- Bangalore street context
- Dark/dimly-lit environments (shows LED/screen glow)

### Graphics
- Cyber-industrial aesthetic
- Dark backgrounds with neon accents
- System diagrams with clean monospace typography
- Terminal/code screenshots with syntax highlighting
- Bounding box overlays on detection images

### Social Media Templates
- **LinkedIn:** 1200×627px, dark bg, white text, cyan accent
- **Instagram Post:** 1080×1080px, dark bg, neon highlights
- **Instagram Story:** 1080×1920px, full-bleed photos with text overlay
- **Instagram Reel:** 1080×1920px, vertical video

---

## Logo Usage Rules

### ✅ Approved
- Black logo on white/light backgrounds
- White logo on dark backgrounds
- Neon cyan glow on cyber-dark backgrounds
- Scaled proportionally

### ❌ Not Approved
- Stretching/distorting
- Rotating
- Adding drop shadows to the logo itself
- Placing on busy/cluttered backgrounds
- Changing logo colors to off-brand colors
- Making the logo smaller than minimum size
