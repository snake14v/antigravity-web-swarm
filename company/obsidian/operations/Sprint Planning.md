## Sprint Rules

### Sprint Duration
- 2 weeks per sprint
- Check-in at midpoint (Vaishak reviews all progress)
- Retrospective at end

### Chunk Sizing
- **S (Small):** 1-2 hours of AI agent work — documentation, small fixes
- **M (Medium):** 2-4 hours — feature building, content creation
- **L (Large):** 4-8 hours — architecture changes, multi-file refactors
- **XL (Extra Large):** 8+ hours — must be split into smaller chunks
