# 📜 Ooru Logix — Company Charter

## Mission Statement
> **Transform Bangalore's unorganized retail sector with AI-powered intelligence that any shop owner can afford and use — no cloud dependency, no monthly SaaS trap, no complexity.**

## Vision
Become the default operating system for India's 12 million+ small retail shops by 2030, starting with Bangalore's hyper-local ecosystem.

## Values

### 1. Local-First, Always
We build for the guy running a chai angadi at 5 AM, not for a VC pitch deck. Every feature must work offline, on slow internet, in a dimly lit shop.

### 2. AI That Serves, Not Surveils
Our AI helps merchants sell more and waste less. We never sell their data. They own their customers. Period.

### 3. Hardware Meets Software
Through our dual entities (Ooru Logix + xgo3d Engineering), we control the full stack — from 3D-printed camera mounts to YOLO model inference.

### 4. Open Source Where Possible
ShopSense core is MIT-licensed. We believe in building in public and letting the community strengthen our code.

### 5. Build With AI, Not Just For AI
Our company itself is run by AI agents. Claude and Antigravity are not tools — they are team members with defined roles, workflows, and memory continuity.

- Not a consulting firm (we ship product)

### What We ARE
- An **AI + Hardware** company that builds intelligence into physical retail spaces
- A **local-first** tech company that understands Bangalore deeply
- An **agent-orchestrated** company where AI does most operational work
