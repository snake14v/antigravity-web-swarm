## Customer Type Reference

- **Grocery/General Store:** High footfall, diverse SKUs
- **Specialty Retail:** Focused category (sweets, bakery, pharmacy)
- **QSR/Café:** Food service, quick service
- **Fashion/Apparel:** Clothing, accessories
- **Electronics:** Gadgets, phones, accessories
