## Market Entry Strategy

### Phase 1 (Months 1-3)
- Focus: JP Nagar + Jayanagar (highest density, established market)
- Target: 5 pilots
- Method: Direct founder sales, referral-driven

### Phase 2 (Months 4-6)
- Focus: Expand to Banashankari, BTM Layout
- Target: 10 pilots total
- Method: Founder sales + early partnerships

### Phase 3 (Months 7-12)
- Focus: Enter Kothnur, optimize all zones
- Target: 20+ pilots
- Method: Sales team, channel partnerships, word-of-mouth

---

## Scouting Notes & Constraints

- **Seasonality:** Retail footfall peaks during festival seasons (Diwali, New Year)
- **Competition:** Many shops already using basic POS or legacy systems
- **Owner accessibility:** Owner availability highest on weekend mornings
- **Location preference:** Main roads with high visibility preferred for sales
- **Budget sensitivity:** Tier-2/3 shops price-sensitive; emphasize ROI
