## Competitive Advantages (Moat Analysis)

### ShopSense Moat

| Advantage | Type | Durability | Details |
|-----------|------|-----------|---------|
| Real-time shelf monitoring | Product | Medium | Edge AI, not replicated by POS incumbents |
| Field accuracy on local products | Data/ML | High | 6+ months of training data per vertical |
| Offline-first edge deployment | Technology | Medium | Runs on Raspberry Pi with local inference |
| Shop manager UX | Product | Low-Medium | Easily matched by competitors with resources |
| Cost structure | Business | Medium | Lower hardware costs than alternative hardware |
| India-first focus | Market | High | Early market share in tier-2/3 cities |

### Defensibility Score: 6.5/10

**Strengths:**
- First-mover advantage in Indian micro-retail
- Unique offline-first architecture
- Low switching costs post-deployment (customer inertia)

**Vulnerabilities:**
- Well-capitalized incumbents can copy product
- No IP/patent moat (standard ML tech)
- Dependent on continuous model improvements
- Network effects not yet established
