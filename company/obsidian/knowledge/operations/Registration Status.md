## Status Reference

| Status | Definition | Action Required |

| Approved | Registered and active | Update all business documents |
| Rejected | Application denied | Review feedback, resubmit with corrections |
| Lapsed | Expired, renewal needed | Initiate renewal process immediately |
| In Review | Under examination by authority | Provide additional docs if requested |

## Required Documentation Checklist

### Sole Proprietorship
- [ ] Identity proof (Aadhar/Passport)
- [ ] Address proof
- [ ] Business concept document
- [ ] Bank account statement or letter from bank

### GST Registration
- [ ] PAN card
- [ ] Address proof
- [ ] Business proof (lease agreement, utility bill)
- [ ] Aadhar card
- [ ] Bank account details

### MSME/Udyam Registration
- [ ] Aadhar number
- [ ] PAN or business registration number
- [ ] Business details (sector, investment, turnover)
- [ ] Bank account details

### Startup India DPIIT
- [ ] Certificate of incorporation or similar
- [ ] Registered business address proof
- [ ] Founder details & background
- [ ] Business plan & innovation details
- [ ] Supporting documentation

### Trademark Registration
- [ ] Brand logo/design (for visual mark)
- [ ] Goods/services classification
- [ ] Applicant identification details
- [ ] Power of attorney (if using agent)
- [ ] Filing fees

### Business Bank Account
- [ ] Business registration documents
- [ ] Proprietor ID proof
- [ ] Address proof
- [ ] Initial deposit requirement
- [ ] Signatory authorization documents
